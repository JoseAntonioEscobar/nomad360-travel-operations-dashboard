# NOMAD360 – Travel Operations Command Center

An interactive operations dashboard designed to demonstrate how data, processes, and operational visibility can be combined to support adventure travel companies operating across South America.

This portfolio project simulates the operational environment of a premium expedition and travel operator, providing insights into traveler readiness, destination performance, partner coordination, financial health, operational risks, and overall expedition execution.

While all data presented is fictional, the workflows, metrics, and operational concepts are inspired by real-world travel operations, logistics coordination, customer support, and project management practices.

---

## Project Overview

NOMAD360 was created as a practical demonstration of how operational information can be transformed into actionable insights through dashboards, process visualization, and KPI-driven management.

The project focuses on the operational side of travel businesses rather than traditional tourism marketing, emphasizing coordination, execution, readiness, risk management, and continuous improvement.

---

## Dashboard Modules

### Command Center
Executive overview of key operational indicators, active expeditions, traveler readiness, and destination performance.

### Traveler Management
Registration tracking, documentation status, passport validation, flight confirmations, insurance verification, and departure readiness monitoring.

### Partner Network
Visibility into destination partners, local operators, service providers, and operational dependencies.

### Trip Financial Health
Revenue tracking, operational performance indicators, margin visibility, and expedition-level financial monitoring.

### Traveler Experience
Customer satisfaction metrics, service quality indicators, and traveler journey monitoring.

### Expedition Readiness
Operational preparedness assessments prior to departure, highlighting potential issues before they become disruptions.

### Risk Intelligence
Identification of operational bottlenecks, delays, documentation risks, and expedition-critical alerts.

### Data Model & DAX
Illustrative analytical layer showing how operational data can be structured and transformed into business insights.

### KPI Catalog
Centralized repository of operational KPIs and performance metrics.

### Visual Blueprint
High-level overview of dashboard architecture and information design principles.
